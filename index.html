Best Movies According to Me

My top 4 movies of all time

Back to the Future

Marty McFly, a 17-year-old high school student, is accidentally sent 30 years into the past in a time-traveling DeLorean invented by his close friend, the maverick scientist Doc Brown.
RATING *****

Hot Tub Time Machine

Three friends, Adam, Nick and Lou, are tired of the problems in their lives and decide to visit a winter resort. Their lives change when they mysteriously teleport to the year 1986.
RATING *****

The Imitation Game

Alan Turing, a British mathematician, joins the cryptography team to decipher the German enigma code. With the help of his fellow mathematicians, he builds a machine to crack the codes for the British government during World War II.
RATING ****

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Upon his arrival in India, Indiana Jones, an American adventurer, is asked by the villagers of a remote hamlet to retrieve a mystical stone and rescue young boys kidnapped from the village.
RATING ****
